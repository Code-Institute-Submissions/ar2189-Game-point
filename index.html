Laserpoint, like laser tag games all over Germany, is still relatively young. The arena is 500 square meters and up to 16 players can be let loose on each other at the same time. The principle is the same as in all other halls: You will be equipped with a tagger and a vest. With these you go into the dark labyrinth and you try to get out with tactics, skill and endurance as the best point!
